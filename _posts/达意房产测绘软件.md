# 新建幢图

## 任务提取

按 usercode 查询，查询出 Flow_Flag = '3' AND State_Flag ='0'

```sql
declare @usercode varchar(50)
set @usercode = N'00000011'

SELECT RTRIM(T.Income_ID) AS Income_ID,Four_Code,Owner_Name,Location,Build_Code,Build_Name,Developer,Ask_Code FROM Reg_Mapping_Main M,Reg_Mapping_Task T WHERE T.Income_ID =M.Income_ID  AND Flow_Flag = '3' AND State_Flag ='0' AND Ask_Code ='01' AND T.Income_ID NOT IN (SELECT Income_ID FROM Dwg_Name) AND T.User_Code = @usercode ORDER BY T.Income_ID DESC
```
